Daubenmire</a></span>\t\t\t\t\t</div><!-- .entry-meta -->\n\n\t\t\t\t\t<div class=\"entry-content\">\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<h3 class=\"post-subtitle subtitle\" style=\"text-align: center;\"><em><strong>I guess O\u2019Reilly\u2019s word of the day is sayonara.</strong></em></h3>\n<p>They say it is not very kind to kick a man when he is down, so I hope that this little ditty doesn\u2019t come across as gloating. That certainly is not my intention.<span id=\"more-71845\"></span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">But after all of the \u201cKilling\u201d books authored by Fox News star <a title=\" Killing O\u2019Reilly \" href=\"http://amzn.to/2pVH5rp\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\">Bill O\u2019Reilly</a>, it seems rather apparent to me that there is a great opportunity for some industrious investigative reporter to author a tell-all book about the downfall of cable news\u2019 biggest star. \u201cKilling O\u2019Reilly\u201d is sure to be a best-seller.</span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">O\u2019Reilly\u2019s show has been a smash hit and certainly shaped the expansion of the alternative media. He trail blazed a new media frontier with his quasi-news/quasi-opinion format to the point it is now accepted behavior for media types to offer more opinion than news. There are many talking heads today who should bow his/her head in thanks that The Leprechaun blazed a new trail down which he enabled the likes of <a title=\" Killing O\u2019Reilly \" href=\"http://freedomoutpost.com/?s=Rachel+Maddow\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\">Rachel Maddow</a>, <a title=\" Killing O\u2019Reilly \" href=\"http://freedomoutpost.com/?s=Megyn+Kelly\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\">Megyn Kelly</a>, <a title=\" Killing O\u2019Reilly \" href=\"http://freedomoutpost.com/?s=Anderson+Cooper\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\">Anderson Cooper</a>, and Al Sharpton to kick up some dust. </span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">But in the end, it will have to be admitted that <a href=\"http://www.dcclothesline.com/2017/04/20/fox-fires-bill-oreilly-murdoch-boys-slash-and-burn/\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\">Bill O\u2019Reilly</a> wasn\u2019t murdered as much as he committed harikari because of his inability to control his overblown ego. A wise man once said that \u201cpride commeth before destruction,\u201d and there can be no doubt that O\u2019Reilly had a double portion of pomposity.</span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">On the air, Mr. O\u2019Reilly loved to spout the line that he \u201cwas looking out for you\u201d when in fact he spent most of his time looking out for him. The whispers of his extracurricular affairs with women have lurked in the shadows for years, and finally, it seems to have reached the point that <a href=\"http://www.dcclothesline.com/2017/04/20/fox-fires-bill-oreilly-murdoch-boys-slash-and-burn/\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\">the Murdoch boys</a> could no longer control the rumors or the sucking sound of greenbacks being evacuated from their coffers.</span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">Yep, it seems as if the one who dutifully warned us five nights a week that \u201cthe spin stops here\u201d was unable to extricate himself from the whirling that was whizzing around him. Those of us with any semblance of discernment realized years ago that old \u201cfair and balanced Bill\u201d was actually neither. I haven\u2019t watched his show in years.</span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">Although his longevity was due in large part to his bombastic approach to his reporting of the news, he was, in many ways, a wolf in sheep\u2019s clothing to the \u201cconservative\u201d Fox News line-up. In fairness to him, he never did call himself a \u201cconservative,\u201d but it seemed rather disingenuous on the part of both him and the management at Fox News to cast a \u201cmoderate\u201d as the flag bearer of the network designed as an alternative to the liberal lamestream media.</span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">Even worse, at least from my chair, was the mixing of his self-proclaimed Catholicism with a liberal position on most social issues. He was moderately pro-life, blatantly pro-homosexual, and a horrible role model for confused Catholics who were struggling to find common ground between their faith and their values. On issues where many conservatives need clarity, O\u2019Reilly was murky at best. In his attempt to be \u201cfair and balanced,\u201d his own moral confusion often bubbled to the surface. There is no such thing as fair and balanced Truth. The Truth is stand alone evidence\u2026Jefferson called it self-evident\u2026and O\u2019Reilly never seemed to find his footing on solid ground when it came to issues of morality.</span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">In the end, his own version of morality is what did him in. </span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">The final straw for me was a few years ago, in the heat of debate over the birth certificate, when I was sitting in my easy chair champing on some freshly popped corn when I heard him utter that his \u201cstaff\u201d had \u201cseen the birth announcements\u201d and that it was legit. </span><a title=\" Killing O\u2019Reilly \" href=\"https://www.liveleak.com/view?i=403_1304929482\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\"><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">Here</span></a><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">. I spilled my bowl of popcorn and my drink into my lap with the audacity of that statement.</span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">It wasn\u2019t that \u201cbirtherism\u201d was such a big deal to me; it was simply that my research had shown that no certificate had ever been produced, but that a copy was available on the White House website. I knew O\u2019Reilly was pretty connected in political circles, but I also knew darn well that neither he nor any member of his staff had seen it.</span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">He doubled-down on the issue a few years later, in spite of the evidence Sheriff Joe Arapio uncovered. </span><a title=\" Killing O\u2019Reilly \" href=\"http://www.wnd.com/2012/03/oreilly-im-too-busy-to-report-on-obama-eligibility/\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\"><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">Read it here</span></a><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">. I never turned him on again.</span></p><div class=\"wpInsert wpInsertInPostAd wpInsertMiddle\" style=\"margin: 5px; padding: 0px;\"><div id=\"ld-2901-1196\"></div><script>(function(w,d,s,i){w.ldAdInit=w.ldAdInit||[];w.ldAdInit.push({slot:8985198184511335,size:[0, 0],id:\"ld-2901-1196\"});if(!d.getElementById(i)){var j=d.createElement(s),p=d.getElementsByTagName(s)[0];j.async=true;j.src=\"//cdn2.lockerdome.com/_js/ajs.js\";j.id=i;p.parentNode.insertBefore(j,p);}})(window,document,\"script\",\"ld-ajs\");</script></div>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">Fox News is now reeling a bit. In the past year, they have lost <a href=\"http://www.dcclothesline.com/2016/09/08/fox-suddenly-yanks-greta-van-susteren-off-the-air-after-14-years-deletes-her-from-foxnews-com/\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\">Greta Van Susteren</a>, <a href=\"http://www.dcclothesline.com/2017/01/04/megyn-kelly-joins-the-dark-side-by-signing-with-leftist-nbc/\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener noreferrer\">Megyn Kelly</a>, and now the kingpin himself. That was the meat of their batting order from 7-10 pm. From what I understand, Tucker Carlson has filled Megan Kelly\u2019s seat admirably, but filling O\u2019Reilly\u2019s shoes will be a tight fit.</span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">But ultimately, it was O\u2019Reilly\u2019s moderation that did him in. His loyal audience of 21 years had grown comfortable with him. His schmoozing up to Obama and eventually Hillary left him vulnerable with his base. When the call for his head finally arrived, there were very few folks willing to go to the mat in an attempt to save the Bloviator\u2019s job. In the end, his audience didn\u2019t love O\u2019Reilly as much as he loved O\u2019Reilly.</span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">The Left was looking for a scalp and they found one in the fair and balanced, compromising Fox News icon. There seems to be a bit of poetic justice in the whole affair, don\u2019t you agree?</span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">It will be interesting to see how this all plays out, but Killing O\u2019Reilly could be a death blow to the ratings at Fox, as well. If they really wanted to pump some energy into their conservative lineup, perhaps they should go out and recruit O\u2019Reilly nemesis Michael Savage. </span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">It was Dr. Savage who nearly two decades ago launched the mantra \u201cborders, language, and culture,\u201d which is nearly identical to the platform that propelled Trump into the White House.</span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">The spin stops here. We\u2019re looking out for you. Fair and balanced news. The No Spin Zone.</span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">Has anyone seen Roger Ailes?
