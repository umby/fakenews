href=\"http://www.dcclothesline.com/2016/02/15/its-not-the-zika-virus-doctors-expose-monsanto-linked-pesticide-as-cause-of-birth-defects/\" target=\"_blank\">Zika virus</a> causes the birth defect called <a href=\"http://www.dcclothesline.com/2016/03/02/leaked-report-petrochemical-pollution-causes-microcephaly-not-zika/\" target=\"_blank\">microcephaly</a>. (Zika archive <a href=\"https://jonrappoport.wordpress.com/category/zika/\" target=\"_blank\"><em>here</em></a>)<span id=\"more-70891\"></span></p>\n<p>Basically, Brazilian researchers, in the heart of the purported \u201cmicrocephaly epidemic,\u201d decided to stop their own investigation and simply assert Zika was the culprit. At that point, they claimed that, out of 854 cases of microcephaly, only 97 showed \u201csome relationship\u201d to Zika.</p>\n<p>You need to understand that these figures actually show evidence AGAINST Zika. When researchers are trying to find the cause of a condition, they should be able to establish, as a first step, that the cause is present in all cases (or certainly an overwhelming percentage).</p>\n<p>This never happened. The correlation between the presence of Zika and microcephaly was very, very weak.</p>\n<p>As a second vital step, researchers should be able to show that the causative virus is, in every case, present in <em>large amounts</em> in the body. Otherwise, there is not enough of it to create harm. MERE PRESENCE OF THE VIRUS IS NOT ENOUGH. With Zika, proof it was present in microcephaly-babies in large amounts has never been shown.</p>\n<p>But researchers pressed on. A touted study in the New England Journal of Medicine claimed Zika infected brain cells in the lab. IRRELEVANT. Cells in labs are not human beings. The study also stated that Zika infected baby mice. IRRELEVANT. Mice are not humans. And these mice in the lab had been specially altered or bred to be \u201cvulnerable to Zika.\u201d USELESS AND IRRELEVANT.</p>\n<p>All this fraud set the stage for the <strong>Zika DNA vaccine</strong>. Yes, it is under development. It is, in fact, an example of the next generation of vaccines. And this is why you should watch out.</p>\n<p>Here is an excerpt from a US National Institutes of Health press release (8/3/16) (<a href=\"https://www.nih.gov/news-events/news-releases/nih-begins-testing-investigational-zika-vaccine-humans\" target=\"_blank\">here</a>, <a href=\"https://www.niaid.nih.gov/news-events/nih-begins-testing-investigational-zika-vaccine-humans\" target=\"_blank\">here</a>, and, the booster to the DNA vaccine <a href=\"https://www.nih.gov/news-events/news-releases/testing-investigational-inactivated-zika-vaccine-humans-begins\" target=\"_blank\">here</a>):</p>\n<p>\u201cThe National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases (NIAID), part of the National Institutes of Health [NIH], has launched a clinical trial of a vaccine candidate intended to prevent Zika virus infection.\u201d</p>\n<p>\u201cScientists at NIAID\u2019s Vaccine Research Center (VRC) developed the investigational vaccine \u2014 called the NIAID Zika virus investigational DNA vaccine \u2014 earlier this year.\u201d</p>\n<p>\u201cThe investigational Zika vaccine includes a small, circular piece of DNA \u2014 called a plasmid \u2014 that scientists engineered to contain genes that code for proteins of the Zika virus. When the vaccine is injected into the arm muscle, cells [in the person\u2019s body] read the genes and make Zika virus proteins, which self-assemble into virus-like particles. The body mounts an immune response to these particles, including neutralizing antibodies and T cells. DNA vaccines do not contain infectious material \u2014 so they cannot cause a vaccinated individual to become infected with Zika \u2014 and have been shown to be safe in previous clinical trials for other diseases.\u201d</p>\n<p>SYNTHESIZED GENES ARE INJECTED INTO THE BODY.</p>\n<p>That\u2019s why it\u2019s called a DNA vaccine.</p>\n<p>Beginning to wonder what this is all about?</p>\n<p>It\u2019s about PERMANENTLY ALTERING YOUR DNA.</p>\n<p>It\u2019s about altering the DNA of every person on the planet who is vaccinated.</p>\n<p>New York Times, 3/9/15, <a href=\"https://www.nytimes.com/2015/03/10/health/protection-without-a-vaccine.html\" target=\"_blank\"><em>\u201cProtection Without a Vaccine.\u201d</em></a> The article describes the frontier of research. Here are key quotes that illustrate the use of synthetic genes to \u201cprotect against disease,\u201d while changing the genetic makeup of humans. This is not science fiction:</p>\n<p>\u201cBy delivering synthetic genes into the muscles of the [experimental] monkeys, the scientists are essentially re-engineering the animals to resist disease.\u201d</p>\n<p>\u201c\u2019The sky\u2019s the limit,\u2019 said Michael Farzan, an immunologist at Scripps and lead author of the new study.\u201d</p>\n<p>\u201cThe first human trial based on this strategy \u2014 called immunoprophylaxis by gene transfer, or I.G.T. \u2014 is underway, and several new ones are planned.\u201d [That was nearly two years ago.]</p>\n<p>\u201cI.G.T. is altogether different from traditional vaccination.
