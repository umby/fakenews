Haiti\u2019s downfall from a tropical paradise resort destination, hundreds of children were left homeless and many of them without parents. This easy prey then attracted the world\u2019s most vile predators.</p>\n<p>More than 300 children have come forward in the last decade with these claims and only a tiny fraction of those accused have ever faced any form of accountability.</p>\n<p>One of the reasons these sickos aren\u2019t charged is because when it comes to keeping its peacekeepers in check, the UN passes the buck.
