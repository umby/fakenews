But now she finds herself at the center or a raging controversy. After 17 years of marriage and after having three children together, Jill and Jeff have decided to divorce because of Jeff\u2019s sexuality.</p>\n<p>For much of their marriage Jeff thought that he was bisexual, but later he came to the conclusion that he was exclusively gay, and that has made their marriage <a title=\"\u201cuntenable\u201d\" href=\"http://www.dailymail.co.uk/femail/article-4293652/Woman-shares-heartbreak-husband-tells-gay.html\" target=\"_blank\">\u201cuntenable\u201d</a>\u2026</p>\n<blockquote><p>A woman has shared her heartbreak after her husband of 17 years told her he is gay.</p>\n<p>Jill and Jeff Smokler, both 39, have three children, aged nine, 11 and 13, recently made the agonizing decision to separate after 23 years together.</p>\n<p>The couple, from Baltimore, Maryland, plan to divorce after Jeff\u2019s suppressed sexuality made their marriage untenable and left him suffering from depression.</p></blockquote>\n<p>Jeff says that he came to the realization that he was exclusively gay four or five years ago, and he claims that he has been suppressing his sexuality since that time. Once I knew, the first and only person I told was my wife. And so began a long, unexpected journey that Jill and I decided we would take together.</p>\n<p>The details of that journey are personal, and they are not mine alone to tell. That journey belongs to us, and it has shaped who we are today as people, as parents, as friends and as lovers.</p>\n<p>But one thing I\u2019ll say about my own journey is this: What should have been an easy choice \u2014 a no brainer \u2014 was the hardest decision I\u2019ve ever made.
