Penney and Macy\u2019s are planning to shut down so many locations in 2017.\u00a0 According to one recent report, 310 shopping malls in America <a title=\"are in imminent danger of losing an anchor store</a>\u2026</p>\n<blockquote><p>Dozens of malls have closed in the last 10 years, and many more are at risk of shutting down as retailers like Macy\u2019s, JCPenney, and Sears \u2014 also known as
