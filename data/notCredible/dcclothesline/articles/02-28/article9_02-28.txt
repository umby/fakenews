At the gate, each passenger had already had their tickets scanned and we were all walking on the jet bridge to board. It\u2019s at this point that most people assume that it is all done: finally we can enjoy some sense of normalcy.</p>\n<p>This time was different. Halfway down the jetbridge, <strong>there was a new layer of security. I guess I could have turned back at the point, decline to take the flight I had paid for, but it would be unclear what would then happen. After standing there for perhaps 8 seconds, the machine gave the go signal and I boarded.</p>\n<p>I talked to a few passengers about this and others were just as shaken by the experience.
