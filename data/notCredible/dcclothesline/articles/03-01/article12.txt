as the new chair of the DNC, Perez was asked about Clinton&#8217;s continued efforts to put her face out and speak politically. Perez did not say that she would not run. Rather, he said she could run if she wanted to.</p>\n<p>\u201cEveryone who wants to run should run,\u201d he <a href=\"http://www.washingtonexaminer.com/perez-doesnt-rule-out-a-hillary-clinton-run-for-the-presidency-in-2020/article/2615807?custom_click=rss\">said</a> of Clinton. \u201cAnd, I\u2019m confident we\u2019re going to have a robust field of candidates.\u201d</p>\n<p>Perez was not really focused on Clinton nor the 2020 race during the interview.
