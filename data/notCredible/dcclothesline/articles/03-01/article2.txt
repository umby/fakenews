Etna <a title=\"can be seen 30 kilometers away\" href=\"http://www.express.co.uk/news/science/773141/Mount-Etna-volcano-BLOWS-Sicily-eruption\" target=\"_blank\">can be seen 30 kilometers away</a>\u2026</p>\n<blockquote><p>Giant fountains of lava could be seen sprouting from the volcano, located on the isle of Sicily, as far away as Catania, around 30 kilometres away, and the resort town of Taormina.</p>\n<p>The Meteorological Observatory in Nunziata said: \u201cYou can clearly see the lava fountains, although currently modest, as it escapes from the crater in the southeast.\u201d</p>\n<p>An orange air alert has been issued, meaning that airspace will remain open but authorities will continue to monitor the situation.</p></blockquote>\n<p>On the other side of the world, a constant stream of molten rock has been springing out of Guatemala\u2019s \u201cVolcano of Fire\u201d <a title=\"since February 25th\" href=\"http://www.straitstimes.com/world/americas/guatemalas-volcano-of-fire-erupts\" target=\"_blank\">since February 25th</a>\u2026</p>\n<blockquote><p>Guatemala\u2019s Volcano of Fire erupted Saturday (Feb 25), spewing lava and sending up plumes of ash that rained down on nearby communities and could eventually reach the capital, civil protection authorities said.</p>\n<p>The Volcan de Fuego, one of the country\u2019s three active volcanoes, is located about 45km southwest of the capital Guatemala City. It was the volcano\u2019s second eruption this year.</p></blockquote>\n<p>And in the middle of the Pacific Ocean, a \u201cfirehose of lava\u201d has been pouring out of the Kilauea Volcano <a title=\"since December 31st\" href=\"https://qz.com/903913/lava-from-a-hawaiian-volcano-continues-to-flow-like-an-unstoppable-firehose-into-the-pacific-ocean/\" target=\"_blank\">since December 31st</a>.</p>\n<p>Meanwhile, a number of large volcanoes that have been dormant for a very long time all over the world have started springing back to life.</p>\n<p>For instance, the only active volcano in India has suddenly started <a title=\"\u201cspewing lava and ash\u201d\" href=\"http://mashable.com/2017/02/20/india-active-volcano-barren-islands-erupts-after-150-years/#8Zsf8KjRvOq8\" target=\"_blank\">\u201cspewing lava and ash\u201d</a> after being silent for 150 years\u2026</p>\n<blockquote><p>Barren Islands volcano, India\u2019s only active volcano, is <a title=\"reportedly\" href=\"http://www.hindustantimes.com/india-news/barren-islands-india-s-only-active-volcano-erupted-in-january-scientists/story-cSNHpoNNEYFhHZOCUIexaP.html\" target=\"_blank\">reportedly</a> spewing lava and ash after a gap of 150 years. It erupted for about four hours in January, scientists from the National Institute of Oceanography (NIO) claimed.</p>\n<p>The volcano is situated in Barren Islands in the Andaman &amp; Nicobar archipelago. Some unsubstantiated reports even claim that it is South Asia\u2019s only active volcano.</p>\n<p>Its first recorded eruption dates back to 1787. Since then, the volcano has erupted more than ten times, including the one this year.</p></blockquote>\n<p>At one time scientists would speak of \u201cdead volcanoes\u201d, but now we learning that it really isn\u2019t safe to speak of any volcano as being completely \u201cdead\u201d.\u00a0 So many of these long dormant volcanoes are roaring back to life, and why this is suddenly happening now is puzzling many of the experts.</p>\n<p>And as you have seen, this isn\u2019t isolated to just one or two geographic regions.\u00a0 It literally is happening all over the globe.</p>\n<p>Last month, Indonesia\u2019s Mount Sinabung in the southern hemisphere erupted seven times <a title=\"in the space of a single day\" href=\"http://www.thebigwobble.org/2017/02/the-tenth-volcano-to-erupt-in-last-week.html\" target=\"_blank\">in the space of a single day</a>, and meanwhile authorities in the northern hemisphere were warning us that four of Iceland\u2019s biggest volcanoes <a title=\"are preparing to erupt\" href=\"http://themostimportantnews.com/archives/four-of-icelands-volcanoes-are-on-the-brink-of-erupting\"
