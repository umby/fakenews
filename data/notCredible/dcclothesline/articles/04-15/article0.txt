had been drawing on, and the things that he had been drawing were so unusual that she decided to ask him about them.</p>\n<p>Well, it turns out that this little boy had been trying to draw a picture of things that were shown to him in a vision.\u00a0 When he received this vision he was in bed, but he was not asleep at the time.\u00a0 He told his grandmother that in his vision he was standing in space and looking down at the Earth, and it appears that he may have been shown things that are coming in the future.\u00a0 If you would like to watch the entire video, you can find it on YouTube <a title=\"here\" href=\"https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yGXdECAjJtA\" target=\"_blank\">here</a>.</p>\n<p><center><iframe src=\"https://www.youtube.com/embed/yGXdECAjJtA\" width=\"560\" height=\"315\" me.\u00a0 He said that he saw a giant asteroid that appeared to be \u201cflaming hot red\u201d coming toward the Earth.</p>\n<p>As it got very close, he said that it appeared to break up into pieces and strike multiple locations, but according to him the main impact seems to have happened in the Atlantic Ocean.</p>\n<p>When this little boy said that I was absolutely startled, because my wife and I have been collecting dreams and visions of a giant asteroid that is going to strike the Atlantic Ocean for many years.\u00a0 In fact, my wife has collected <a title=\"more than 100\" href=\"http://whygodreallyexists.com/archives/a-collection-of-asteroid-and-tsunami-dreams\" target=\"_blank\">more than 100</a> of these dreams and visions.\u00a0 Many have seen that the asteroid breaks up into multiple pieces before it hits, and that the impact in the Atlantic Ocean causes an enormous tsunami that will be hundreds of feet high.\u00a0 When this gigantic tsunami slams into the east coast of the United States at an extremely high speed, the devastation that will be caused will be almost too horrible for words.</p>\n<p>As you can see in <a title=\"the video\" href=\"https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yGXdECAjJtA\" target=\"_blank\">the video</a>, this little boy is not even allowed on the Internet, and so he probably has never even heard about any of these dreams and visions.\u00a0 But his description of the vision that he was given matches up perfectly with what dozens of other prominent men and women of God have received over the years.</p>\n<p>And of course the Bible tells us that someday a giant object will plunge into the ocean.\u00a0 The following is what Revelation 8:8-9 says <a title=\"in the Modern English Version\" second angel sounded, and something like a great mountain, burning with fire, was thrown into the sea.
