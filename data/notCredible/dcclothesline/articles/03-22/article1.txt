British surveillance agents to conduct surveillance on Donald Trump\u2019s team prior to the election</a>.</p>\n<p>Napolitano has not been on the air since Thursday and is not expected to be on the news network anytime in the near future, sources told the LA Times.</p>\n<p>President Trump accused Obama of ordering his \u201c\u2018wires tapped\u2019 in Trump Tower just before the victory\u201d in a tweet on March 4.</p>\n<blockquote class=\"twitter-tweet\" data-width=\"550\">\n<p lang=\"en\" dir=\"ltr\">Terrible!
