href=\"https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NHPeZ08BW6M\" target=\"_blank\">Liam Scheff</a></p></blockquote>\n<p><span id=\"more-71421\"></span></p>\n<p><a href=\"http://www.dcclothesline.com/2015/09/30/this-1926-eugenics-exhibits-sums-up-what-the-elite-think-about-you-and-your-family-2/\" target=\"_blank\">Eugenics</a> in\u00a0one form or another has always been a part of human culture, however\u00a0it has taken a quantum leap since the 1800\u2019s when it was first codified into science by <a href=\"https://www.amazon.com/gp/search/ref=as_li_qf_sp_sr_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=wakitime09-20&amp;keywords=Francis%20Galton&amp;index=aps&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;linkId=f79123f38b9f221d46c3fc3c4029e203\" target=\"_blank\">Francis Galton</a>. Now, in the 21st century, <a href=\"http://www.dcclothesline.com/2014/12/14/century-ago-rockefellers-funded-eugenics-initiative-sterilize-15-million-americans/\" target=\"_blank\">eugenics</a> is broadly accepted to be immoral and unethical, yet is disguised in\u00a0many aspects of our society, namely vaccination.</p>\n<blockquote><p>\u201cWe live in a society in which eugenics is prohibited, a society that it doesn\u2019t believe in abortion, we certainly don\u2019t believe in infanticide, but we seem to be killing a lot of children, anyway. We seem to be killing a lot of children slowly.\u201d ~<a href=\"https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NHPeZ08BW6M\" target=\"_blank\">Liam Scheff</a></p></blockquote>\n<p>Speaking on the connection between eugenics and vaccines, and how families, most notably mothers, are destroyed when a child is vaccine injured, <a href=\"http://liamscheff.com/\" target=\"_blank\">Liam Scheff</a>, anti-vaccine activist and author of\u00a0<a href=\"https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/147756134X/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=wakitime09-20&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;creativeASIN=147756134X&amp;linkId=a87e4123aaeaa08e4f27931e726d7c29\" target=\"_blank\">Official Stories: Counter-Arguments for a Culture in Need</a>\u00a0describes how the\u00a0practice and ritual of vaccinating children qualifies as a eugenics project.</p>\n<p><a href=\"https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eugenics\" target=\"_blank\">Eugenics is defined</a> as, \u201cthe science of improving a human population by controlled breeding to increase the occurrence of desirable heritable characteristics.\u201d</p>\n<p>According to Scheff:</p>\n<blockquote><p>\u201cVaccination is a eugenics project. That is it is meant to improve the health of the species before sickness arrives. The eugenics movement has a history of killing and wounding many in the large populations to which the \u2018science\u2019 is applied. The vaccine movement, as part of the eugenic movement, is now killing families across the world and the United States.\u201d\u00a0~<a href=\"https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NHPeZ08BW6M\" target=\"_blank\">Liam Scheff</a></p></blockquote>\n<p>A quote from the father of scientific population control, <a href=\"https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1490385738/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=wakitime09-20&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;creativeASIN=1490385738&amp;linkId=4ffb2fc8f602481ca86ce0c661b2b4e3\" target=\"_blank\">Thomas Malthusian</a>, serves as a reminder of the true intentions of some scientists and social engineering programs:</p>\n<blockquote><p>\u201cInstead of recommending cleanliness to the poor, we should encourage contrary habits.
