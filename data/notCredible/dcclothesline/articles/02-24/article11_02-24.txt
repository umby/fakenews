Congress Do Not Repeal Obamacare?</h1>\n\n\t\t\t\t\t<div class=\"entry-meta\">\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<span class=\"meta-prep meta-prep-author\">Posted on</span> <a href=\"http://www.dcclothesline.com/2017/02/24/how-angry-will-you-be-if-the-republicans-in-congress-do-not-repeal-obamacare/\" title=\"2:01 am\" rel=\"bookmark\"><span class=\"entry-date\">February 24, 2017</span></a> <span class=\"meta-sep\">by</span> <span class=\"author vcard\"><a class=\"url fn n\" href=\"http://www.dcclothesline.com/author/michael-snyder/\" title=\"View all posts by Michael Snyder\">Michael Snyder</a></span>\t\t\t\t\t</div><!-- .entry-meta -->\n\n\t\t\t\t\t<div class=\"entry-content\">\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<p><img class=\"aligncenter size-large wp-image-11853\" src=\"https://i1.wp.com/theeconomiccollapseblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/Obamacare-Report-Card-Facebook-460x252.jpg?resize=460%2C252\" alt=\"Obamacare Report Card - Facebook\" data-recalc-dims=\"1\" />Top Republicans are now publicly saying that Obamacare will never be fully repealed. In fact, many Republicans in Congress are already using the term \u201crepair\u201d instead of \u201crepeal\u201d to describe what is going to happen to Barack Obama\u2019s signature healthcare law. Without <span id=\"more-69900\"></span>a doubt, the Republicans in Congress are eventually going to do <strong>something</strong>, but strategists in both parties are now suggesting that most of the key elements of Obamacare are going to remain once everything is all said and done. It will be put into a more \u201cconservative\u201d package, but it will still be Obamacare.</p>\n<p>On Thursday, former House Speaker John Boehner made headlines all over the country when he said that a complete repeal of Obamacare is <a title=\"\u201cnot what\u2019s going to happen\u201d\" href=\"http://www.politico.com/story/2017/02/john-boehner-obamacare-republicans-235303\" target=\"_blank\">\u201cnot what\u2019s going to happen\u201d</a>.\u00a0 Instead, Boehner said that Republicans are going to \u201cfix Obamacare\u201d and that they will \u201cput a more conservative box around it\u201d in order to keep their constituents happy.</p>\n<p>Of course this isn\u2019t what we voted for. For years, Republican politicians all across the country have been promising that Obamacare would be repealed once they got control of Congress, but now Boehner is telling us that all of that was just <a title=\"\u201chappy talk\u201d\" href=\"http://www.politico.com/story/2017/02/john-boehner-obamacare-republicans-235303\" target=\"_blank\">\u201chappy talk\u201d</a>\u2026</p>\n<blockquote><p>Earlier in the panel discussion, Boehner said he \u201cstarted laughing\u201d when Republicans started talking about moving lightning fast on repeal and then coming up with an alternative.</p>\n<p>\u201cIn the 25 years that I served in the United States Congress, Republicans never, ever, one time agreed on what a health care proposal should look like. Not once,\u201d Boehner said. <strong>\u201cAnd all this happy talk that went on in November and December and January about repeal, repeal, repeal\u2014yeah, we\u2019ll do replace, replace\u2014I started laughing, because if you pass repeal without replace, first, anything that happens is your fault. You broke it.\u201d</strong></p></blockquote>\n<p>When the Republicans finally get around to doing something, they will inevitably declare it to be a great victory, but will it actually be that much different from what we have now?</p>\n<p>Yes, the IRS penalty for not having health insurance will probably go. But there will still be coverage for children up to the age of 26, there will still be mandatory coverage for preexisting conditions, there will still be mandatory coverage for maternity expenses, there will still be some form of Medicaid expansion and there will still be subsidies for the poor.</p>\n<p>In the end, we are still going to have a healthcare system where half the country pays for the healthcare for the other half of the country.</p>\n<p>That isn\u2019t fair, and it never will be.\u00a0 One half of the country shouldn\u2019t have to pay much higher rates for their own health insurance and also pay for the healthcare of everyone else in the nation as well.\u00a0 Either we should go back to a free market system, or they might as well go ahead and socialize the entire thing.</p>\n<p>The thought of sticking with what we have right now is utter insanity, but unfortunately that is what top Republicans mean when they speak of \u201crepairing\u201d Obamacare. The following comes from <a title=\"the New York Times\" href=\"https://www.nytimes.com/2017/02/23/us/politics/obamacare-affordable-care-act-house-republicans.html\" target=\"_blank\">the New York Times</a>\u2026</p>\n<blockquote><p>\u201cWhen you talk about \u2018repeal,\u2019 you have just used a word that is very polarizing,\u201d said Representative Tom MacArthur, Republican of New Jersey, who meets weekly with moderate Republicans and Democrats of equal number. <strong>\u201cWhen you go to Democrats and say, \u2018Help us repeal,\u2019 that puts them in a box. If you say, \u2018Would you help us repair something?\u2019 people start listening in a whole other way.\u201d</strong></p></blockquote>\n<p>How in the world do you \u201crepair\u201d a steaming pile of garbage?</p>\n<p>I just don\u2019t understand.</p>\n<p>What the Republicans need to do is very simple. As <a title=\"Jim Demint\" href=\"http://www.rollcall.com/news/politics/demint-to-congress-repeal-obamacare-now\" target=\"_blank\">Jim Demint</a> has suggested, the Republicans in Congress simply need to pass the same Obamacare repeal that Barack Obama vetoed not too long ago\u2026</p>\n<blockquote><p>Heritage Foundation President <a class=\"memberLink\" title=\"Click to view member info in a new window\" href=\"http://data.rollcall.com/members/439?rel=memberLink\" target=\"_blank\" shape=\"rect\">Jim DeMint</a>, the former South Carolina Republican senator, called on activists attending the Conservative Political Action Conference to push their members of Congress to send to President Donald Trump the same legislation that dismantled the law and was vetoed by President <a class=\"memberLink\" title=\"Click to view member info in a new window\" href=\"http://data.rollcall.com/members/7612?rel=memberLink\" target=\"_blank\" shape=\"rect\">Barack Obama</a> with all due haste.</p>\n<p>\u201cWe must and we can repeal Obamacare now,\u201d DeMint said. <strong>\u201cThey should send that same bill to President Trump right now.\u201d</strong></p></blockquote>\n<p>So what is keeping Republicans in Congress from moving forward?</p>\n<p>One thing is the defunding of Planned Parenthood. Some liberal Republicans are promising to vote against any Obamacare repeal bill <a title=\"that defunds Planned Parenthood\" href=\"http://thehill.com/policy/healthcare/320764-gop-sen-wont-vote-to-defund-planned-parenthood-casts-doubt-on-repealing\" target=\"_blank\">that defunds Planned Parenthood</a>\u2026</p>\n<blockquote><p>Sen. Lisa Murkowski (R-Alaska) says she will not vote for an ObamaCare repeal bill that defunds Planned Parenthood.</p><div class=\"wpInsert wpInsertInPostAd wpInsertMiddle\" style=\"margin: 5px; padding: 0px;\"><div id=\"ld-2901-1196\"></div><script>(function(w,d,s,i){w.ldAdInit=w.ldAdInit||[];w.ldAdInit.push({slot:8985198184511335,size:[0, 0],id:\"ld-2901-1196\"});if(!d.getElementById(i)){var j=d.createElement(s),p=d.getElementsByTagName(s)[0];j.async=true;j.src=\"//cdn2.lockerdome.com/_js/ajs.js\";j.id=i;p.parentNode.insertBefore(j,p);}})(window,document,\"script\",\"ld-ajs\");</script></div>\n<p>In her address to Alaska\u2019s state legislature Wednesday, the moderate Republican offered her firmest commitment yet that she will not support defunding Planned Parenthood.</p>\n<p><strong>\u201cI, for one, do not believe that Planned Parenthood has any place in our deliberations on the Affordable Care Act,\u201d</strong> she said.</p></blockquote>\n<p>Another thing that is giving some Republicans pause are the <a title=\"angry protesters\" href=\"http://www.nj.com/politics/index.ssf/2017/02/will_progressive_tea_party_wave_wash_over_nj_repub.html\" target=\"_blank\">angry protesters</a> that they are running into at town hall meetings\u2026</p>\n<blockquote><p>U.S. Sen. Charles Grassley of Iowa and Reps. Jason Chaffetz of Utah, Marsha Blackburn of Tennessee and Tom McClintock of California are among Republicans who faced hostile audiences at recent town hall meetings.</p>\n<p>This comes after the <a title=\"Women\u2019s March on Washington\" href=\"http://www.nj.com/politics/index.ssf/2017/01/trump_on_womens_march_we_just_had_an_election.html\" target=\"_blank\">Women\u2019s March on Washington</a> that drew hundreds of thousands of protestors the day after President Donald Trump\u2019s <a title=\"inauguration\" href=\"http://www.nj.com/politics/index.ssf/2017/01/trumps_inaugural_address_you_will_never_be_ignored_again.html\" target=\"_blank\">inauguration</a>.</p>\n<p>\u201cRepublicans need to be paying attention and doing their best to understand the energy from the town halls,\u201d said Nathan Gonzales, editor and publisher of <a title=\"Inside Elections\" href=\"http://insideelections.com/\" target=\"_blank\">Inside Elections</a>, a Washington-based publication that tracks congressional races.</p></blockquote>\n<p>Of course a lot of those \u201cangry protesters\u201d are from <a title=\"Barack Obama\u2019s private army of more than 30,000 volunteers\" href=\"http://themostimportantnews.com/archives/sabotage-obama-is-commanding-an-army-of-30000-anti-trump-activists-from-his-home-2-miles-from-the-white-house\" target=\"_blank\">Barack Obama\u2019s private army of more than 30,000 volunteers</a> that are being deployed around the nation in a desperate attempt to defend Obamacare.</p>\n<p>In the end, the truth is that the Republicans should be listening to the voters that sent them to Washington in the first place. Most of those voters expected an immediate Obamacare repeal, and now that it has not happened it is making for a very confusing tax season. The following comes from <a title=\"Politico\" href=\"http://www.politico.com/story/2017/02/obamacare-taxpayers-confusion-repeal-235313\" target=\"_blank\">Politico</a>\u2026</p>\n<blockquote><p>Republicans\u2019 stalled campaign to repeal the Affordable Care Act is sowing confusion among those now trying to do their taxes.</p>\n<p>Many taxpayers believe Republicans have already repealed the law, tax preparers say, and they\u2019re surprised and upset to learn they are still subject to Obamacare\u2019s penalty for failing to have health insurance \u2014 a charge that climbed this year to more than $2,000 per family.</p></blockquote>\n<p>Until it is repealed, Obamacare will continue to kill jobs and will continue to <a title=\"kill the middle class\" href=\"http://theeconomiccollapseblog.com/archives/as-predicted-obamacare-is-absolutely-killing-the-middle-class\">kill the middle class</a>.</p>\n<p>It was one of the worst pieces of legislation ever written, and it boggles the mind that so many Republicans in Congress are hesitant about repealing it.</p>\n<p>Unfortunately, just as I portray in <a title=\"my novel\" href=\"http://amzn.to/2mdfbcT\" target=\"_blank\">my novel</a>, America is rapidly going crazy.</p>\n<p>We have been given over to a reprobate mind, and our leaders can\u2019t even seem to think straight any longer.</p>\n<p>If Obamacare is going to be repealed, now is the time.
