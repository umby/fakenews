This has no intelligence value and let me be clear on this. I\u2019ve run these sorts of operations before. I\u2019ve had to go to the White House and brief on this sort of operation and you instantly bin information related to US persons. It used to take us time, like minutes. Now it can be done instantly, I mean the moment it hits, so there\u2019s absolutely no technical reason.
