class=\"author vcard\">usapoliticsnow admin</span></span></div>\t</header>\n\t\n\t<div class=\"entry-content\">\n\t\t<div id=\"contentad280488\"></div>\n<p>Evidence that the document presented to the American public by Barack Obama as his Hawaiian birth certificate is fake soon will be heading to members of Congress, and the difficult discussions over what crimes may have been committed and what should be done could begin soon.</p>\n<p>The atmosphere in Washington will be different under Trump, who will be inaugurated Jan. 20, since he publicly questioned Obama\u2019s birth certificate in 2011. During this election season,\u00a0he stated he now\u00a0believes Obama was born in Hawaii.</p>\n<p>But at a\u00a0<a href=\"http://wnd.com/2016/12/investigators-find-source-for-obamas-online-birth-certification-image/\">news conference Thursday, Maricopa County, Arizona, Sheriff Joe Arpaio,</a> the only law enforcement officer to formally investigate the allegations of fraud, presented evidence that\u00a0the\u00a0image of the birth document posted by the White House was\u00a0fraudulent.</p>\n<p>One thing seems certain: The evidence will have to go elsewhere for action to be taken, since Arpaio, known as \u201cAmerica\u2019s toughest sheriff\u201d and innovator of such jail amenities as pink underwear and surplus Army tents in the desert heat for jail inmates, is leaving office Jan. 1 after more than two\u00a0decades serving Phoenix-area\u00a0residents, who had asked him for the birth certificate investigation.</p>\n<p>His replacement already has stated, <a href=\"http://www.cbsnews.com/news/arizona-sheriff-joe-arpaio-replacement-paul-penzone-tough-odd-choices/\">CBS News reported,</a> that he \u201cwill cut off Arpaio\u2019s investigation into Obama\u2019s birth certificate.\u201d</p>\n<p>Mike Zullo, Arpaio\u2019s lead investigator since 2011\u00a0on the birth certificate issue, told WND he wants to get the evidence to members of Congress for evaluation. It could support a Presidential Transparency Act, which would give secretaries of state across the nation the authority to investigate candidates with suspect qualifications.</p>\n<p>\u201cThis is what we have learned. Every secretary of state is powerless to investigate a candidate,\u201d he told WND. \u201cThey have to take everything on face value.\u201d</p>\n<p>That means a candidate whose qualifications are questioned cannot be reviewed by state secretaries of state, who\u00a0run the elections</p>\n<p>Zullo told WND that could be a very easy fix for the controversy that developed around Obama\u2019s document.</p>\n<p>The issue is that the U.S. Constitution requires the president to be a \u201cnatural-born citizen\u201d but does not define the term. Scholarly works cited by the Founders defined it as a citizen at birth, born in the country to two citizens of the country, or merely the offspring of two citizens of the country.</p>\n<p>The birth certificate Obama displayed on the White House website as \u201cproof positive\u201d of his eligibility states he was born in Hawaii to an American mother and a Kenyan father.</p>\n<p>Some immediately pointed out alleged anomalies, questioning its validity, while others argued it also could prove his ineligibility because his father was not a citizen. Some of the lawsuits over the issue argued Obama was a dual citizen at birth through his father, contending the framers of the Constitution excluded dual citizens from qualifying as natural born citizens.</p>\n<p>Eventually, just as <a href=\"http://superstore.wnd.com/Wheres-the-Birth-Certificate-Autographed-Hardcover?promocode=story\">the No. 1 bestseller \u201cWhere\u2019s The Birth Certificate?\u201d</a> was combining with a challenge at that time from billionaire businessman Donald Trump to force Obama\u2019s hand, Obama held a White House news conference to release a copy of his birth certificate.</p>\n<p>Now, what has been described as the only official law enforcement investigation ever done into the Obama birth certificate has concluded it is \u201cnot authentic.\u201d</p>\n<p>Jerome Corsi, Ph.D., WND senior staff writer and author of \u201cWhere\u2019s the Birth Certificate?\u201d was credited by sheriff\u2019s officials with contributing to the
