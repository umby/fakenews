Amen! All of our veterans deserve praise, but it\u2019s great that Vietnam Veterans are finally starting to earn the praise that they deserve.</p>\n<p>God bless our vets:</p>\n<p><iframe width=\"740\" height=\"555\" src=\"https://www.youtube.com/embed/Qp0LYc4IHnY?feature=oembed\" frameborder=\"0\" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>\n<p><strong>*** Let\u2019s say a prayer for all of our surviving Vietnam Veterans!\u00a0</strong></p>\n<p>This bill was co-sponsored by Sen.
