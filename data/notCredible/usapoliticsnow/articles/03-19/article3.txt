Democratic Party has morphed into a distinctly anti-American party representative only of the globalist movement, two legislators have submitted a bill that would effectively neuter the First Amendment.</span></p>\n<p class=\"p1\"><span class=\"s1\">As reported by investigative reporter Tyler Durden of <a href=\"http://www.zerohedge.com/news/2017-03-16/new-york-assemblyman-unveils-bill-suppress-non-government-approved-free-speech\"><span class=\"s2\">zerohedge</span></a>, <i>\u201cthe </i><a href=\"http://nyassembly.gov/leg/?default_fld=&amp;leg_video=&amp;bn=A05323&amp;term=&amp;Summary=Y&amp;Text=Y\"><span class=\"s3\"><b><i>bill aimed at securing a \u201cright to be forgotten,\u201d</i></b></span></a><b><i> </i></b><a href=\"http://nyassembly.gov/leg/?default_fld=&amp;leg_video=&amp;bn=A05323&amp;term=&amp;Summary=Y&amp;Text=Y\"><b><i>New York Assembly Bill 5323</i></b></a>, introduced by <a href=\"http://assembly.state.ny.us/mem/David-I-Weprin/\">Assemblyman David I. Weprin</a> and (as <a href=\"http://nyassembly.gov/leg/?term=2017&amp;bn=S04561\">Senate Bill 4561</a> by <a href=\"https://www.nysenate.gov/senators/tony-avella\">state Sen. Tony Avella</a>),</span> <span class=\"s1\">would</span> <span class=\"s1\"><b><i>require people to remove \u2018inaccurate,\u2019 \u2018irrelevant,\u2019 \u2018inadequate\u2019 or \u2018excessive\u2019 statements about others</i></b><i>\u2026</i></span></p>\n<p class=\"p1\"><span class=\"s1\"><b><i>\u201cFailure to comply would make the search engines or speakers liable for, at least, statutory damages of $250/day plus attorney fees</i></b><i>.\u201d</i></span></p>\n<p class=\"p1\"><span class=\"s1\">The fact that these two fascists would even float this discussion is frightening proof of just how extreme Democrats have become in defense of big government and their globalist agenda.</span></p>\n<p class=\"p1\"><span class=\"s1\">Eugene Volokh, writing in the Washington Post, was appalled:</span></p>\n<p class=\"p1\"><span class=\"s1\"><i>\u201cSo, under this bill, newspapers, scholarly works, copies of books on Google Books and Amazon, online encyclopedias (Wikipedia and others) </i><b><i>\u2014 all would have to be censored whenever a judge and jury found (or the author expected them to find) that the speech was \u201cno longer material to current public debate or discourse\u201d</i></b><i> (except when it was \u201crelated to convicted felonies\u201d or \u201clegal matters relating to violence\u201d in which the subject played a \u201ccentral and substantial\u201d role). And of course </i><b><i>the bill contains no exception even for material of genuine historical interest; after all, such speech would have to be removed if it was \u201cno longer material to current public debate.\u201d</i></b><i> Nor is there an exception for autobiographic material, whether in a book, on a blog or anywhere else. Nor is there an exception for political figures, prominent businesspeople and others.</i></span></p>\n<p class=\"p1\"><span class=\"s1\"><b><i>\u201cBut the deeper problem with the bill is simply that it aims to censor what people say, under a broad, vague test based on what the government thinks the public should or shouldn\u2019t be discussing.\u201d</i></b></span></p>\n<p class=\"p1\"><span class=\"s1\">[\u2026]</span></p>\n<p class=\"p1\"><span class=\"s1\"><i>\u201cRemember: There is no \u201cright to be forgotten\u201d in the abstract; no law can ensure that, and no law can be limited to that. Instead</i><b><i>, the \u201cright\u201d this aims to protect is the power to suppress speech \u2014 the power to force people (on pain of financial ruin) to stop talking about other people, when some government body decides that they should stop.\u201d</i></b></span></p>\n<p class=\"p1\"><span class=\"s1\">Lest you find comfort in the fact that this bill originated in New York \u2013 a bastion of left-wing lunacy \u2013 remember what was contained in the National Defense Authorization Act that recently passed the lame duck Congress and was signed into law by Barack Obama that <a href=\"http://www.patriotcrier.com/breaking-obama-just-singed-bill-nullifying-1st-amendment-happening/\"><span class=\"s2\">funded the establishment of a Global Engagement Center containing wording sufficiently vague enough for the GEC to become a Ministry of Truth.</span></a></span></p>\n<p class=\"p1\"><span class=\"s1\">The wording from this section of the NDAA goes hand in hand with Congress\u2019 silence as <a href=\"http://www.patriotcrier.com/clinton-obama-set-criminalize-political-dissent/\"><span class=\"s2\">Barack Obama turned control of the Internet over to global control</span></a>.</span></p>\n<p class=\"p1\"><span class=\"s1\">This should frighten every patriotic American who cherishes the concept of inalienable rights that no government can take away.</span></p>\n<p class=\"p1\"><span class=\"s2\"><a href=\"http://www.viralliberty.com/hillary-clinton-vows-shut-dissenting-media-elected/\">Remember Hillary Clinton</a></span><span class=\"s1\"> openly declaring that when she became president Breitbart would be silenced?<span class=\"Apple-converted-space\">\u00a0 </span>How different might things for the future of our country had Hillary been given the power to appoint the tie-breaking vote to the Supreme Court?</span></p>\n<p class=\"p1\"><span class=\"s1\">If legislation like this were to become the law just imagine how a court similar in political orientation with the Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals would abuse its power to end political debate and dissent in our country.<span class=\"Apple-converted-space\">\u00a0 </span>It would, in fact, give unelected judges the right to strike the writings of our Founding Fathers from our texts so that any analysis of the principles that formed the foundation for our country\u2019s
