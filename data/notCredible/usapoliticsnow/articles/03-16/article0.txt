by <span class=\"author vcard\">usapoliticsnow admin</span></span></div>\t</header>\n\t\n\t<div class=\"entry-content\">\n\t\t<div id=\"contentad280488\"></div>\n<p>Most people think that the two most important names in the Democratic race for the White House in 2016 are Obama and Clinton. However, the two names you should be paying attention to are Cloward and Piven.</p>\n<p>Don\u2019t know who they are? Don\u2019t worry, most people don\u2019t \u2014 but conservative commentator and generally kicka** guy Chuck Norris is going to make sure they do.</p>\n<p>In an op-ed for WND, Norris explained that the two people \u2014 Richard Cloward and Frances Fox Piven \u2014 were academics who came up with a plan to introduce permanent socialism in the United States.</p>\n<p>Norris said that \u201cthe Cloward-Piven strategy is a political plan created in 1966 by two Columbia University sociologists to overload the U.S. public welfare system, the goal being to replace it with a national system of \u2018a guaranteed annual income and thus an end to poverty.\u2019</p>\n<p>\u201c<strong>I echo again that it\u2019s neither a coincidence that Obama graduated from Columbia University nor that others who espoused the Cloward-Piven strategy were a group of radicals who have been a part of his life and education: Bill Ayers, Saul Alinsky, Bernardine Dohrn, George Wiley, Frank Marshall Davis, Wade Rathke and George Soros, among others</strong>,\u201d Norris continued.</p>\n<p>\u201c<strong>What\u2019s also not a coincidence is how close Hillary ran in the same circles.</strong>\u201d</p>\n<p>Norris then goes on to make the frightening connections.</p>\n<p>\u201c<strong>Robert Chandler, a retired Air Force colonel and former strategist for the White House \u2026 explained in the Washington Times that \u2018much of (the Cloward-Piven) strategy was drawn from Saul Alinsky.\u2019 There\u2019s no doubt that Alinsky\u2019s life and work served as the coach and plan for Cloward and Piven,</strong>\u201d Norris writes.</p>\n<p>\u201c<strong>Hillary\u2019s similar discipleship devotion was clearly seen by her 92-page college thesis paper on Saul Alinsky, which is a glimpse behind the present veil of her politically savvy and guarded gloss into the heart of her true ideologies \u2026 No surprise that in 1993, the Clintons asked officials at her alma mater to hide Hillary Rodham\u2019s senior thesis from Clinton biographers.</strong>\u201d</p>\n<p>Norris also notes that the \u201c<strong>Motor Voter</strong>\u201d law, which was signed by Bill Clinton, was the brainchild of Cloward and Piven.</p>\n<p>Barbara Ehrenreich, a collaborator of Cloward and Piven\u2019s, wrote that the movement for such a law \u201cwas dedicated to the idea that if citizens were allowed to register to vote when they apply for aid from government programs or for drivers\u2019 licenses, some of the historic administrative encumbrances on the right to vote could be overcome.</p>\n<p>\u201c<strong>The poor in particular, who often lack the time for voter registration, would be effectively enfranchised</strong>.\u201d</p>\n<p>And that\u2019s not all.</p>\n<p>\u201c<strong>What\u2019s not surprising is how the \u2018Motor Voter\u2019 law has morphed to incorporate more Cloward-Piven-Clinton strategies,</strong>\u201d Norris wrote.</p>\n<p>\u201c<strong>For example in Oregon, legislators have just enacted a subsidiary law that registers a person to vote when they obtain or renew a state driver\u2019s license. That might sound all fine and dandy, too, until one realizes that a stone\u2019s throw away in neighboring states like California, they are doling out licenses to 1.5 million illegals over the next three years. How long will it be until California\u2019s Motor Voter laws extend voting rights to illegals, too?</strong>\u201d</p>\n<p>\u201c<strong>A first-year political student can easily see where a Hillary presidency will take Cloward-Piven strategies</strong>,\u201d Norris concluded.</p>\n<p>\u201c<strong>You see, the plan to \u2018fundamentally transform the United States of America\u2019 has a successor, and she must be stopped.</strong>\u201d</p>\n<p><strong>Share</strong> if you <strong>AGREE</strong>\u00a0that Hillary must be stopped.</p>\n<div id=\"contentad265941\"></div>\n<div
