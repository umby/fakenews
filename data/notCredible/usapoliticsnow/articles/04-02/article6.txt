families who face those challenges. During today\u2019s press briefing, Sean Spicer announced that the White House will be lit in blue on Sunday to honor this cause!</p>\n<blockquote class=\"twitter-tweet\" data-width=\"550\">\n<p lang=\"en\" dir=\"ltr\">On Sun, <a href=\"https://twitter.com/WhiteHouse\">@WhiteHouse</a> will honor World Autism Awareness Day &amp; <a href=\"https://twitter.com/hashtag/LightItUpBlue?src=hash\">#LightItUpBlue</a> to join w/ the autism awareness community <a href=\"https://t.co/oz7Hf2Tzbu\">https://t.co/oz7Hf2Tzbu</a></p>\n<p>&mdash; Sean Spicer (@PressSec) <a href=\"https://twitter.com/PressSec/status/847880204680699904\">March 31, 2017</a></p></blockquote>\n<p><script async src=\"//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js\" charset=\"utf-8\"></script></p>\n<p>Here\u2019s more information about how you can help support this amazing cause:</p>\n<p>From <a href=\"http://www.autism-society.org/\" target=\"_blank\">Autism-society.org:</a></p>\n<p><em><strong>Put on the Puzzle! </strong>The Autism Awareness Puzzle Ribbon is the most recognized symbol of the autism community in the world. Autism prevalence is now one in every 68 children in America. Show your support for people with autism by wearing the Autism Awareness Puzzle Ribbon \u2013 as a pin on your shirt, a magnet on your car, a badge on your blog, or even your Facebook profile picture \u2013 and educate folks on the potential of people with autism! To learn more about the Autism Awareness Ribbon, <a href=\"https://www.autism-society.org/about-the-autism-society/history/autism-awareness-ribbon/\" target=\"_blank\">click here</a>. To purchase the Autism Awareness Puzzle Ribbon for your shirt, car, locker or refrigerator, <a href=\"https://dsiresources.com/?id=e696e3df-78d1-4790-84b4-0058684cb8c3\" target=\"_blank\">click\u00a0here</a>.</em></p>\n<p><em><strong>Connect with your neighborhood.</strong> Many Autism Society <a href=\"http://www.autismsource.org/\" target=\"_blank\">local affiliates</a> hold special events in their communities throughout the month of April. But if you can\u2019t find an event that suits you just right, <a href=\"http://autismsociety.ngpvanhost.com/ngpvanui/login?destination=ngpvansocialorg/58/home\" target=\"_blank\">create your own</a>!<br />\n</em><em><strong><br />\nWatch a movie.</strong> Did you know that something that seems as simple as going to the movies is not an option for many families affected by autism? The Autism Society is working with <a href=\"https://www.amctheatres.com/programs/sensory-friendly-films\" target=\"_blank\">AMC Theatres</a> to bring special-needs families <a href=\"https://www.autism-society.org/get-involved/events/sensory-friendly-films/\" target=\"_blank\">Sensory Friendly Films</a> every month.</em></p>\n<p><em><a href=\"https://autism-society.ngpvanhost.com/form/-4866437795647780864\" target=\"_blank\"><strong>Donate to the Autism Society:</strong></a> Help improve the lives of all impacted by autism with a financial gift to the Autism Society.
