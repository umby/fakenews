again.</p>\n<p>Despite the persistent claim by those on the left that voter fraud doesn&#8217;t exist, a liberal activist from Ohio pleaded guilty to 14 counts of voter fraud on Monday. Rebecca A. Hammonds, who was originally charged with 35 counts, was sentenced to six months in prison.</p>\n<p>Hammonds, a paid Ohio Organizing Collaborative canvasser, was charged with \u201cfalsely registering people to vote and forging signatures on voter registration forms&#8221; in Columbiana County, reports The American Mirror.</p>\n<p>\u201cIt was fraud. It was dozens of forms where people that were deceased or just forged flat out,&#8221; said Adam Booth, Columbiana County elections director. &#8220;They signed their name and it wasn\u2019t even the voter, the wrong date of birth, wrong streets.&#8221;</p>\n<p>Booth said the criminal was caught in the fraud scam because his officer took the right precautions, making sure &#8220;all the information matches up, from their first name, middle name, last name, to all the identifiers.\u201d</p>\n<p>Associate assistant attorney general Brian Deckert suspects Hammonds committed fraud out of &#8220;human laziness,&#8221; as he noted that she wasn&#8217;t paid by signature, but at an hourly rate.</p>\n<p>It wasn&#8217;t &#8220;a situation where she had a quota system,&#8221; said Deckert. &#8220;I think it just may have come down to human laziness.\u201d</p>\n<p>Hammonds was facing up to one year in prison per count of voter fraud, but was only sentenced to six months.</p>\n<p>\u201cI just can\u2019t overlook this. You attempted to violate the integrity of our election process in the county,\u201d said residing Judge C. Ashley Pike.</p>\n<p>\u201cYou made a mockery of our system,&#8221; added Judge Pike. &#8220;If 180 days [in the county jail] doesn\u2019t teach you a lesson, nothing will.\u201d</p>\n<p>Democrats have long been suspected of committing voter fraud, and have been quick to call foul whenever conservatives bring up the serious issue, essentially labeling such fraud as a myth used to enact &#8220;racist&#8221; laws which would require individuals to show a simple form of photo ID before voting. But as James O&#8217;Keefe&#8217;s Project Veritas videos revealed during the 2016 election, voter fraud does indeed occur, often as a coordinated effort by those on the left.</p>\n<p>&#8220;According to Democratic operative Scott Foval, who works for top Democratic operative Bob Creamer, operatives rent cars to drive to different sites,&#8221; explained Daily Wire Editor-in-Chief Ben Shapiro, referring to O&#8217;Keefe&#8217;s videos outlining the voter fraud. &#8220;Foval said, &#8216;you can\u2019t prove conspiracy.&#8217; Foval explained, &#8216;You know what? We\u2019ve been busing people in to deal with you f***ing a******s for 50 years, and we\u2019re not going to stop now.'&#8221;</p>\n<p>Damning.</p>\n<p>Watch O&#8217;Keefe&#8217;s video on Democratic
