class=\"entry-header clearfix\"><h1 class=\"entry-title\">Hospital CEO Sues CNN For False Reporting, Wins BIG TIME\u2026</h1><p class=\"mh-meta entry-meta\">\n<span class=\"entry-meta-date updated\"><i class=\"fa fa-clock-o\"></i><a href=\"http://success-street.com/2017/03/\">March 7, 2017</a></span>\n<span class=\"entry-meta-author author vcard\"><i class=\"fa fa-user\"></i><a class=\"fn\" href=\"http://success-street.com/author/andrej/\">Jamie</a></span>\n<span class=\"entry-meta-categories\"><i class=\"fa fa-folder-open-o\"></i><a href=\"http://success-street.com/category/news/\" rel=\"category tag\">News</a></span>\n<span class=\"entry-meta-comments\"><i class=\"fa fa-comment-o\"></i><a class=\"mh-comment-scroll\" href=\"http://success-street.com/2017/03/07/hospital-ceo-sues-cnn-false-reporting-wins-big-time/#mh-comments\">0</a></span>\n</p>\n\t</header>\n\t\t<div class=\"entry-content clearfix\">\n<figure class=\"entry-thumbnail\">\n<img src=\"https://i2.wp.com/success-street.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/CNN-1.jpg?resize=640%2C361\" alt=\"\" title=\"CNN-1\" />\n</figure>\n<div id=\"contentad284953\"></div>\n<p>CNN purposefully skewed infant-mortality rate statistics that cost a hospital CEO his job \u2014 and the judge did not mince words when it comes to the \u201cfake news\u201d being peddled from the network.<span id=\"more-1511\"></span></p>\n<div class=\"mh-content-ad\">\u00a0This win comes as Judge\u00a0Orinda Evans outright rejected CNN\u2019s bid to get the case dismissed, which continues the legal process.</div>\n<div class=\"mh-content-ad\">\n<p>Davide Carbone, former CEO of St. Mary\u2019s Medical Center in West Palm Beach, filed a defamation lawsuit against CNN after they aired what he claims were a \u201cseries of false and defamatory news reports\u201d regarding the infant mortality rate at the hospital. CNN\u2019s report said the mortality rate was three times the national average. However, Mr. Carbone contends that CNN \u201cintentionally\u201d manipulated statistics to bolster their report. He also claims that CNN purposely ignored information that would look favorable to the hospital in order to sensationalize the story.</p>\n<p>\u201cIn our case, we contended that CNN essentially made up its own standard in order to conduct an \u2018apples to oranges\u2019 comparison to support its false assertion that St. Mary\u2019s mortality rate was 3 times higher than the national average. Accordingly, the case against CNN certainly fits the description of media-created \u2018Fake News.\u2019\u201d said Carbone\u2019s attorney L.
