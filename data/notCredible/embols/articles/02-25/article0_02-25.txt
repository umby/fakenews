The results of that race will certainly determine the future of the Democrat Party. But one thing is for certain, if the Democrat Party no longer believes in national sovereignty, yet believes their raison d\u2019\u00eatre is about telling us who goes to what bathroom, that is not a winning proposition. We\u2019re all watching these manic town hall meetings happening at GOP member town halls \u2014 many are comparing this to the rise of the constitutional conservative grassroots movement known as the Tea Party. But there\u2019s a big difference. The folks yelling at these GOP members are bereft in understanding the intricacies of policy. One person yelling, \u201cObamacare saved my life\u201d \u2014 ok, that\u2019s a very kind emotional screech, but there are hundreds of thousands of other Americans who lost their doctors and insurance coverage, which Barack Obama said would not happen.</p>\n<p>I\u2019d love to sit and have the discussion with any of these well-rehearsed and scripted shouters about what the \u201crisk corridor\u201d was in Obamacare. Or how about the twenty new taxes Obamacare brought upon the American people \u2014 one as insidious as the venerable tanning tax, and we shared with y\u2019all an analysis of the consequences of that tax on female small business owners</p>\n<p>Maybe we can talk about how Obamacare destroys Medicare Part D as a bill payer, or how many insurance companies are dropping Obamacare coverage because the costs are just too high. Perhaps we could have a nice chat about a national debt that went from $10.67 trillion to over $19.5 trillion in the eight years of the Obama reign \u2014 and that\u2019s supposed to be fiscal success? Oh yeah, part of that included the massive spending junkets of Obamacare and the stimulus \u2014 which only stimulated the excessive debt growth.</p>\n<p>Yes, I could easily go on ad nauseam on policy issues where the past eight years of a progressive socialist ideological policy agenda failed this nation. And no amount of screaming, car burning, violence, and protests can change facts\u2026as a liberal Democrat senator from New York, Daniel Patrick Monyihan, once quipped, \u201cyou are entitled to your own opinions, but not your own facts.\u201d And we know facts to liberal progressive socialists are like sunlight to vampires. Hence why the political contest in Atlanta for the leadership of the DNC is all about more emotion, more movement to the far left, and not about connecting to America\u2019s fundamental principles and values.</p>\n<p>And therein lies the huge difference between what happened with the Tea Party movement and this conclave of emotional rantings that the left is trying to create\u2026it ain\u2019t based on any policy fact; just empty rhetoric and emotion. It\u2019s truly grounded in a sense of political coercion and intimidation, and it does appear to be working if you consider the GOP majority recalcitrance to \u201cmake it happen.\u201dLet\u2019s be honest, when the left is in power, they don\u2019t give a damn what constitutional conservatives think\u2026and anyway, we don\u2019t \u201cfeel\u201d as a primary motive. The left, i.e. Obama, told his political opposition that they won, and rammed their agenda down America\u2019s throat. And after eight years, that agenda was repudiated and rejected, which REALLY angered the left who feels that anyone not agreeing with their vision is stupid, ignorant, racist, sexist, and suffering from multiple phobias.</p>\n<p>And oddly enough, even after losing, based on policy failure, the left continues to feel the same way. Well, good luck with that. My advice to the House and Senate GOP is, borrowing from the Nike maxim, \u201cJust Do It.\u201d If for one single minute you believe you\u2019re going to appease and make the New York Times, Washington Post, LA Times, CNN, NBC, MSNBC, or any other leftist media outlet happy and like you, well, you\u2019ve been smoking crack.</p>\n<p>Now is not the time to be concerned with what that minority of coastal elitists feel; do what\u2019s right by America, and restore a pro-economic growth and pro-national security agenda by way of constitutional conservative principles and values. Oh yeah Paul Ryan and Kevin Brady, a Border Adjustment Tax just isn\u2019t consistent with those values\u2026I shared a missive about that earlier.</p>\n<p>That brings me to the other major political event happening this weekend in the DC area \u2014 the annual gathering of the Conservative Political Action Committee or conference, known as CPAC.</p>\n<p>Today, in just a few hours, President Trump will address this gathering, and all eyes will be upon him. It is imperative that President Trump articulates what his philosophy of governance is for America. Just as a platform of comparison, Barack Obama\u2019s was rooted in wealth redistribution, nationalization of production, expansion of the welfare nanny-state, social egalitarianism, and secular humanism through executive fiat (just ask Hobby Lobby, Little Sisters of the Poor, or the Kleins of Oregon).</p>\n<p>What President Trump should do this day on this grand stage at the National Harbor of CPAC is clearly delineate why progressive socialism fails, always has and always will. He needs to draw that comparison that speaks to every American, and put the left on defense to explain every single policy failure of the past eight years, and even before.</p>\n<p>This cannot be about Trump populism, but about American conservatism and if Trump knows a little about political philosophy, he\u2019ll tell the world about what classical liberalism is, as opposed to this post-modern perversion that\u2019s been hijacked and called liberalism.</p>\n<p>Here should be the plan: in a short 45 minute address, President Donald Trump could destroy the platform of the left and render them running and frothing. This cannot be another rant about the media, but take the time to educate the American people \u2014 since you do own the stage \u2014 and hammer away at what does indeed \u201cMake America Great Again.\u201d CPAC can be the launching point for a new resurgence of constitutional conservatism in America\u2026and Trump\u2019s address there could set the stage for his address before the joint session of Congress next week. There\u2019s no better way to go on offense and direct the narrative.</p>\n<p>America, whether or you know it, this weekend represents a clear opportunity to make a choice \u2014 well, actually, solidify the choice made last November. As we go forward, it\u2019s time for conservatives to stop living in the realm of fear of the left. And I mean no \u00a0longer fear the specter of George Soros or the influence of entertainment and media elites.</p>\n<p>It\u2019s time to draw the ideological battle lines and embrace the motto of the U.S. Army, \u201cThis We\u2019ll Defend\u201d\u2026but you MUST know that which you are defending. And you must understand that against which you are making a stand.</p>\n<p>This leftist movement, and today\u2019s Democrat Party have moved farther and farther away from the essence of what this Constitutional Republic means. And the only way they can regain their power is not through honest and factual debate, but rather through the suppression of opposing thought, along with violence, coercion, and intimidation. They are taking to the town hall meetings not with a policy solution but with an anger. And it\u2019s a different angst than what was evidenced in the constitutional conservative grassroots movement which said these policies will fail\u2026and fail they did, and will.</p>\n<p>Ya know what I find perplexing? It\u2019s how the progressive socialist left cares so much about those who disregard our national sovereignty \u2014 illegal aliens \u2014 more than they do our veterans. I shall ask the rhetorical question: when did you see the left take to the streets and protest the treatment of our men and women who\u2019ve served this nation, but were horrifically treated by their own Veterans Administration? You didn\u2019t. And that speaks volumes as to the difference.</p>\n<p>CPAC and the DNC Chairman election, happening together, to determine the future of our Republic. Simple choice America \u2014 progressive socialism or constitutional conservatism\u2026true classical liberalism?
