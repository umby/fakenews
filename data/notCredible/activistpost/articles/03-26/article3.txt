Without exemptions there is no informed-consent.\u00a0Without informed-consent there is no choice. Without choice there are no Rights. Without Rights there is no freedom. Yet\u00a0there is always your power of consent \u2013 to accept or deny, to tolerate or not.</p>\n<p>The government manipulates public opinion and engineers consent though messengers such as Erwin Chemerinsky, Dean of the University of California, Irvine school of law and adviser to California\u2019s vaccine mandate law, SB 277 . Those in power turn the Constitution, as a Charter of Freedom, on its head.</p>\n<p class=\"wp-caption-text\" style=\"text-align: center;\"><a href=\"http://www.activistpost.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/We-The-People-685x342.jpg\" rel=\"attachment wp-att-150889\"><img class=\"aligncenter wp-image-150889 size-full\" src=\"http://www.activistpost.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/We-The-People-685x342.jpg\" alt=\"\" width=\"685\" height=\"342\" srcset=\"http://www.activistpost.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/We-The-People-685x342.jpg 685w, http://www.activistpost.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/We-The-People-685x342-300x150.jpg 300w\" sizes=\"(max-width: 685px) 100vw, 685px\" /></a><em>The Constitution</em></p>\n<p>The Constitution is supposed to be a metaphorical shield to protect individual Rights from oppressive forces whose goal is to abolish them. According to the late\u00a0<a href=\"https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jbh-FKLO5t0\">Professor Harry Jaffa</a>:</p>\n<blockquote><p>The constitution exists to protect individual liberty and individual property. And the foundation of all rights are the rights to private property. The right to private property is a right of each individual human being to own himself.</p></blockquote>\n<p>The Constitution is an instrument limiting government. However, according to Professor <a href=\"https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7Ln5K9l0L68\">Thomas DiLorenzo</a>, today, no one applies the Constitution as a limit on governmental power:</p>\n<blockquote><p>The only time republicans talk about the Constitution is whenever they want to use it to block something the democrats are trying to do. And the only time you ever hear a democrat \u00a0talking about the Constitution is when they want to try to use it try to block something the republicans are trying to do.</p></blockquote>\n<p>Today, individuals\u00a0appear to have no protections or Rights. Three separate branches of government (Executive, Legislative, Judicial) are one. There are no checks and balances. From 1937 to 1955, the Supreme Court did not declare a single law as unconstitutional. And it continues. While two sides play tug of war with the Constitution, American freedom is caught in the crosshairs.</p>\n<h3>Open To Interpretation</h3>\n<p><div class=\"alignright\"><!-- /9716847/AP-Content -->\r\n<div id='div-gpt-ad-1470694951173-5' style='height:250px; width:300px;'>\r\n<script>\r\ngoogletag.cmd.push(function() { googletag.display('div-gpt-ad-1470694951173-5'); });\r\n</script>\r\n</div></div>How can the Constitution\u00a0be open to such a wide range of interpretation?</p>\n<p>One\u00a0word, \u201cProperty.\u201d At the signing of the Constitution, People equaled Property equaled slavery. Slavery was not only tolerated in the Constitution, it was codified as law.</p>\n<p>The Constitution of 1787 did\u00a0not mention slavery because it was an accepted practice of the times. Slavery was lawful in every one of the 13 colonies. Even Abraham Lincoln said that racial slavery was Constitutionally implied, but not expressed. The Constitution of 1787 is, therefore, a \u201cslave-holder\u2019s Constitution.\u201d Slavery is referenced in the Constitution in a\u00a0<a href=\"http://www.usconstitution.net/consttop_slav.html\">few key places</a>\u00a0(the Enumeration Clause, Article 1, Section 9,\u00a0The Fugitive Slave Clause).</p>\n<p>Opponents to a free America, such as Alexander Hamilton and others (the original Goldman Sachs), saw that the Constitution could further be interpreted as a rubber stamp to anything the federal government wanted to do under the idea of \u201cImplied Powers.\u201d Hamilton also invented the expansive interpretations of the General Welfare and Commerce Clauses to control the economy. He saw that if you install enough government judges on the Supreme Court who are like-minded, the intent of the Constitution is destroyed.</p>\n<h3>No Room For Interpretation</h3>\n<p>However, there is another Charter created to protect freedom. This document\u00a0is completely ignored by those who would destroy the intent of the Constitution. Its content leaves no room for interpretation. It is a key to unlocking freedom for all people.</p>\n<p><img class=\"alignnone size-full wp-image-3524 aligncenter\" src=\"http://www.natureofhealing.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/49c70da675f5a96cdba866cf0215bc0c.jpg\" sizes=\"(max-width: 480px) 100vw, 480px\" srcset=\"http://www.natureofhealing.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/49c70da675f5a96cdba866cf0215bc0c.jpg 480w, http://www.natureofhealing.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/49c70da675f5a96cdba866cf0215bc0c-150x150.jpg 150w, http://www.natureofhealing.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/49c70da675f5a96cdba866cf0215bc0c-300x300.jpg 300w\" alt=\"49c70da675f5a96cdba866cf0215bc0c\" width=\"480\" height=\"480\" /></p>\n<p>The Declaration of Independence\u00a0does not contain the word \u201cProperty.\u201d In 1776, Thomas Jefferson changed the phrasing of John Locke\u2019s trinity of \u201cLife, Liberty, and Property\u201d by replacing \u201cproperty\u201d with\u00a0<a href=\"http://historynewsnetwork.org/article/46460\">\u201cthe pursuit of happiness,\u201d</a>\u00a0(even though Jefferson\u2019s own \u201cproperty\u201d included about two hundred human beings whom he prevented from\u00a0pursuing their own happiness).</p>\n<p>At the official end of the Civil War, with the\u00a0formal\u00a0<a href=\"http://www.slate.com/blogs/the_vault/2015/04/06/surrender_at_appomattox_grant_s_surrender_terms_drafted_by_ely_parker.html\">surrender of the Confederacy</a>\u00a0at Appomattox Courthouse, the Declaration was codified into law.\u00a0Racial slavery ended. The Declaration made clear that \u201cAll Men Are Created Equal\u201d\u00a0(even though women were not yet considered equal to men).</p>\n<p>On November 19, 1863, Lincoln reiterated the birth of freedom in his Gettysburg Address\u00a0when he said:</p>\n<blockquote><p>Four score and seven years ago\u2026 this nation, under God, shall have a new birth of freedom\u2014and that government of the people, by the people, for the people, shall not perish from the earth.</p></blockquote>\n<p>Four score and seven years ago is the date of\u00a0July 4th 1776, the DECLARATION \u2013 not the Constitution of September 17, 1787. This was no accident. Lincoln showed his commitment when he\u00a0reintroduced the greenback national currency, independent from the power of the Central banks. That act would bring an end to both Lincoln and a free currency.</p>\n<h3>Economic Slavery Made Legal</h3>\n<p>After Lincoln\u2019s death, the only thing standing in the way of the Hamiltonian mindset of control was the Declaration of Independence. Without the Declaration, many in power believed that \u201cslavery\u201d remained legal under the Constitution. Only\u00a0<em>racial</em>\u00a0slavery was illegal.</p>\n<p><a href=\"http://activistpost.net/familysurvival.html\" rel=\"nofollow\"><img class=\"alignright\" src=\"https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/-ASIFGy_z7zU/UCFiDQK8GBI/AAAAAAAAROU/7AHejVY_hUE/s202/3.gif\" /></a>Under Hamilton\u2019s vision,\u00a0economic slavery needed to be\u00a0expanded and made legal through the courts. In\u00a01866, case law ensured that the Declaration would be invalidated. In <strong><em>Santa Clara County v. Southern Pacific Railroad Company, </em></strong>the Supreme Court held that the Fourteenth Amendment of the Constitution granted corporations Rights above persons. In other words, Rights equal Money. Those who had more money had more Rights.</p>\n<p>A later opinion,\u00a0<a href=\"http://caselaw.findlaw.com/us-supreme-court/08-205.html\"><em>Citizens United v. FEC</em></a>, reinforced this. The result is that People = Property. In <em>Bush v. Gore</em>, the majority opinion echoed that the \u201caverage citizen\u201d has no Constitutional Right to elect the POTUS and that this Right rests firmly with Electors. No Inherent Rights, only State-granted Rights.</p>\n<h3>Everyone A Slave</h3>\n<p>The result today is that everyone is a slave. Yet some slaves are \u201cmore equal\u201d than others due to pedigree, privilege, and connections. State-granted privileges replace Inherent Rights under the facade\u00a0of democracy and freedom. Under government\u2019s interpretation, no one has Rights \u2013 only the perception of having Rights.</p>\n<p>Today, America resembles\u00a0a police state, an Oligarchy. If you disagree, ask yourself why the courts continue to reject\u00a0<a href=\"http://www.sandiegouniontribune.com/sdut-vaccination-order-judge-sabraw-2016aug26-htmlstory.html\">appeals</a>\u00a0and\u00a0<a href=\"https://newrepublic.com/article/120950/courts-have-upheld-governments-constitutional-right-vaccine-laws\">constitutional challenges</a>\u00a0to vaccine mandates? Why do\u00a0<a href=\"http://medicalkidnap.com/2016/02/25/child-kidnapping-and-trafficking-a-lucrative-u-s-business-funded-by-taxpayers/\">Child Protection Services (CPS) remove children</a>\u00a0from the home if parents have a natural Right\u00a0to raise their children? Why are peaceful\u00a0<a href=\"http://thehill.com/homenews/state-watch/321018-state-legislators-take-steps-to-criminalize-protests\">protests shut down</a>\u00a0if the First Amendment protects free speech? Because in the eyes of the law People equals Property and State-granted Rights are privileges. If the State holds the power to grant a privilege, the state also holds the power to remove it.</p>\n<p>We can be assured that no State official\u00a0will act on the words\u00a0penned by Thomas Jefferson in the Declaration of Independence to \u201cdissolve the political bands\u201d and restore freedom:</p>\n<blockquote><p>That whenever any Form of Government becomes destructive of these ends, it is the right of the People to alter or to abolish it, and to institute new Government\u2026</p></blockquote>\n<h3>The Power of Consent</h3>\n<p>Individuals can reclaim freedom through the power of consent. Freedom is lost\u00a0when individuals fail to withdraw consent. By asking permission to\u00a0<a href=\"http://vaccineresistancemovement.org/?p=11806\">\u2018opt out\u2019\u00a0</a>from any mandate issued by the State, you inadvertently grant your Government de facto authority over your body.</p>\n<p>While the State claims it has a <em>prima facie </em>duty to \u201cprotect\u201d the public from the morbidity and mortality of communicable diseases, through mandatory\u00a0vaccination, we must ask, \u201cBy what authority and by whose consent?\u201d Today, those in power use the Constitution as an excuse to restrain people and to police the world. The intended purpose of the Constitution has always been to restrain the government.</p>\n<p><a href=\"http://activistpost.net/vaccineexempt.html\" rel=\"nofollow\"><img class=\"alignright\" src=\"https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/-WfI77BHRfFg/TpHdZMZJHeI/AAAAAAAALS4/KDabWFqA1jM/s200/AVGLE2.0-CD.jpg\" alt=\"\" /></a>A little research will show there is no constitutional right to be protected by government. In 1856, the U.S. Supreme Court (<em><a href=\"https://supreme.justia.com/cases/federal/us/59/396/case.html\">South v. Maryland</a></em>) found that law enforcement officers had no duty to protect any individual. Their duty is to enforce the law in general. This was reinforced n 1982 (<a href=\"http://www.leagle.com/decision/19801228486FSupp742_11112/BOWERS%20v.%20DeVITO\"><em>Bowers v. DeVito</em></a>), the Court of Appeals, Seventh Circuit, and again in 2005 <em>(<a href=\"https://www.law.cornell.edu/supct/html/04-278.ZO.html\">Castle Rock v. Gonzales</a>, 545</em> U.S. 748).</p>\n<p>The role of government is to preserve liberty and freedom (the right to choose), as well as to protect private property rights and the general welfare (i.e., border security). The role of government is not to run lives of the people, or the economy under the General Welfare Clause. The people do not consent.</p>\n<h3>Interpret This!</h3>\n<p>Everything written is open to interpretation. Freedom is not open to interpretation. Once something is forced through mandates, choice becomes obsolete. Without \u201cinformed-consent\u201d the \u201cneed to know\u201d no longer applies.</p>\n<p>If Lincoln were alive today, he might\u00a0ask us to see beyond his vision. A new birth of freedom now requires us to see that freedom is not found in any document or Charter. Freedom does not require codification into law. Freedom is\u00a0not found in any court of opinion.</p>\n<p><img class=\"alignnone size-full wp-image-3542 aligncenter\" src=\"http://www.natureofhealing.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/17078576_10155889684079676_181283797_n.jpg\" sizes=\"(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px\" srcset=\"http://www.natureofhealing.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/17078576_10155889684079676_181283797_n.jpg 500w, http://www.natureofhealing.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/17078576_10155889684079676_181283797_n-300x189.jpg 300w\" alt=\"17078576_10155889684079676_181283797_n\" width=\"500\" height=\"315\" /></p>\n<p style=\"text-align: center;\"><em>Declaration of Independence</em></p>\n<p>True freedom is embodied in each of us as a\u00a0state of mind and a way of being. Freedom flows from our humanity by birth, granted by\u00a0the Creator, not by the State. The Declaration of Independence refers to God as \u201cnatural law or nature\u2019s law, unalienable\u2026and the Creator.\u201d Freedom equals Private Property equals the Individual.
