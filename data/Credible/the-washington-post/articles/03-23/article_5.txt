(Those 65 or older are typically eligible for help under Medicare.) The amendment does not spell this out, however, and the House GOP leadership is hoping the Senate would further alter the legislation to include this help.</p><p class=\"pg-bodyCopy\"><em>[<a href=\"https://www.washingtonpost.com/powerpost/trump-to-visit-capitol-tuesday-urge-house-republicans-to-vote-for-health-bill/2017/03/20/4d93f9ac-0d8c-11e7-9b0d-d27c98455440_story.html\">\nHouse Republicans unveil changes to their health-care bill</a>]</em>\n        \n    </p>\n\n<div class=\"pg-divider pg-skinny\">\n    <span class=\"pg-divider-line\"></span>\n    <span class=\"pg-divider-tick\"></span>\n    <span class=\"pg-divider-line\"></span>\n</div>\n\n</div>\n\n            </div>\n        \n            <div class=\"block\" data-block-type=\"change\">\n                \n                <div class=\"change\" id=\"change-4\" data-for=\"left\">\n    <div class=\"head\">\n        \n        <div class=\"text\">\n            <h4 class=\"kicker\">For moderates</h4>\n            <h3 class=\"pg-h3\">Increased inflation factor for elderly and disabled</h3>\n        </div>\n        \n            <div class=\"arrow\">\n                <p>AHCA</p>\n                <img src=\"img/arrow-left.svg\" />\n            </div>\n        \n    </div>\n\n    <p class=\"pg-bodyCopy\">\n        \n            Under the legislation, the way the government helps pay for Medicaid would be profoundly changed. Instead of covering a fixed percentage of the costs of each person on the Medicaid rolls in a given state, the government would begin to pay a fixed sum of money per person, with the sum changing from year to year based on the rate of medical inflation.
