Negotiating a prenup in just a few weeks instead of over the course of several months, as is usually the case, can be tricky, Shemin said, and if it&rsquo;s proven to be done under duress, the agreement won&rsquo;t hold up in court.</p><p>Having a prenuptial agreement could also potentially complicate the process of getting a green card, said Boston immigration lawyer Joshua Goldstein, who said he talks to people &ldquo;all the time&rdquo; who are considering moving up their wedding plans because of Trump&rsquo;s policies.
