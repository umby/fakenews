;\">Boston taught me the tough lesson that racism was more than a treasonous flag snapping in a stiff breeze.</p><p id=\"U832043373969BnH\" style=\" letter-spacing: -5; ;\">In the 1980s, Bradlees, a now-defunct department store, had a policy of noting its customers&rsquo; race on personal checks, presumably so they could undergo further scrutiny.
