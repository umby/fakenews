But she says it&#8217;s her very conservatism that makes her believe government should permit women to terminate pregnancies.</p>\n<p>A self-described &#8220;constitutional conservative,&#8221; Lahren is popular for her often controversial commentary on the right-wing site\u00a0<a href=\"http://www.theblaze.com/\" target=\"_blank\"><em>The Blaze</em></a>. There, she hosts a series of videos taking down liberal issues &#8212; she&#8217;s <a href=\"http://college.usatoday.com/2016/07/13/over-56000-people-want-tomi-lahren-fired-from-the-blaze-after-kkk-tweet/\" target=\"_blank\">called Black Lives Matter &#8220;the new KKK,&#8221;</a> vocalized strong support for Donald Trump <a href=\"http://www.personaldefenseworld.com/2016/10/tomi-lahren-interview-theblaze/\" target=\"_blank\" target=\"_blank\">and for gun rights</a>.</p>\n<div class=\"spike\">Related: <a href=\"http://college.usatoday.com/2016/12/02/5-things-to-know-about-tomi-lahren/\" target=\"_blank\">5 things to know about Tomi Lahren</a></div>\n<div class=\"embed-twitter\">\n<blockquote class=\"twitter-tweet\" data-width=\"550\">\n<p lang=\"en\" dir=\"ltr\">Great time on <a href=\"https://twitter.com/TheView\" target=\"_blank\">@TheView</a> <a href=\"https://t.co/atdhwK0oBl\" target=\"_blank\">pic.twitter.com/atdhwK0oBl</a></p>\n<p>&mdash; Tomi Lahren (@TomiLahren) <a href=\"https://twitter.com/TomiLahren/status/842775863380918272\" target=\"_blank\">March 17, 2017</a></p></blockquote>\n<p><script async src=\"//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js\" charset=\"utf-8\"></script></div>\n<p>After discussing President Trump&#8217;s campaign rhetoric and the travel ban with the co-hosts of <em>The View</em>, the conversation switched to women&#8217;s rights, where Lahren showed a different side. Much like her outspoken commentary against liberal ideologies, Lahren had no trouble calling out those within her own party.</p>\n<p>She said:</p>\n<blockquote><p>\u201cI\u2019m pro-choice, and here\u2019s why. I\u2019m someone that\u2019s for limited government. So I can\u2019t sit here and be a hypocrite and say I\u2019m for limited government but I think the government should decide what women do with their bodies. I can sit here and say that, as a Republican and I can say, you know what, I\u2019m for limited government, so stay out of my guns, and you can stay out of my body, as well.\u201d</p></blockquote>\n<p>Despite her stance on abortion, Lahren has been actively vocal about<a href=\"https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=016rTDVY48A\" target=\"_blank\"> the problems she has with feminism</a>. She recently\u00a0<a href=\"https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Beno3rWXQHE\" target=\"_blank\">spoke out against the Women&#8217;s March</a> due to its exclusion of pro-life groups.
