class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"96\" data-total-count=\"96\">The ninth week of Donald Trump\u2019s presidency began with the F.B.I. director calling him a liar.</p><p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"351\" data-total-count=\"447\">The director, the <a href=\"https://www.lawfareblog.com/james-comey-hillary-clinton-and-email-investigation-guide-perplexed\">very complicated</a> James Comey, didn\u2019t use the L-word in his congressional testimony Comey serves at the pleasure of the president, after all. But his meaning was clear as could be. Trump has repeatedly accused Barack Obama of wiretapping his phones, and Comey explained there is \u201cno information that supports\u201d the claim.</p><p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"453\" data-total-count=\"900\">I\u2019ve previously <a href=\"https://www.nytimes.com/2017/01/06/opinion/lies-journalism-and-objectivity.html?_r=0\">argued</a> that not every untruth deserves to be branded with the L-word, because it implies intent and somebody can state an untruth without doing so knowingly. George W. Bush didn\u2019t lie when he said Iraq had weapons of mass destruction, and Obama didn\u2019t lie when he said people who liked their current health insurance could keep it. They made careless statements that proved false (and they deserved much of the criticism they got).</p><p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"437\" data-total-count=\"1337\">But the current president of the United States lies. He lies in ways that no American politician ever has before. He has lied about \u2014 among many other things \u2014 Obama\u2019s <a href=\"https://www.nytimes.com/2016/09/17/us/politics/donald-trump-obama-birther.html\">birthplace</a>, John F. href=\"https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/fact-checker/wp/2016/03/30/trumps-claim-that-the-u-s-pays-the-lions-share-for-nato/?utm_term=.ae1dd59a6895\">NATO</a>, military <a href=\"https://www.washingtonpost.com/politics/trump-said-he-raised-6-million-for-vets-now-his-campaign-says-it-was-less/2016/05/20/871127a8-1d1f-11e6-b6e0-c53b7ef63b45_story.html?utm_term=.678e88b70c31\">veterans</a>, Mexican <a href=\"https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/fact-checker/wp/2015/07/08/donald-trumps-false-comments-connecting-mexican-immigrants-and-crime/?utm_term=.3ddaf40b25d6\">immigrants</a>, Muslim <a href=\"https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/fact-checker/wp/2016/08/16/fact-checking-donald-trumps-major-speech-on-the-islamic-state/?utm_term=.660dfa3b60a7\">immigrants</a>, anti-Semitic <a href=\"https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/the-fix/wp/2017/02/28/trump-is-reportedly-hinting-that-anti-semitic-incidents-are-false-flags-it-wouldnt-be-the-first-time/?utm_term=.4c8b68f94bbe\">attacks</a>, the <a href=\"http://www.politifact.com/truth-o-meter/statements/2016/feb/11/donald-trump/donald-trump-repeats-pants-fire-claim-unemployment/\">unemployment rate</a>, the <a href=\"https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/fact-checker/wp/2017/01/27/president-trumps-first-seven-days-of-false-claims-inaccurate-statements-and-exaggerations/?utm_term=.3dec2a331bf1\">voter fraud</a> and his <a href=\"http://www.thedailybeast.com/articles/2016/10/12/all-of-donald-trump-s-accusers-a-timeline-of-every-alleged-grope-and-assault.html\">groping of women</a>.</p><p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"218\" data-total-count=\"1555\">He tells so many untruths that it\u2019s time to leave behind the textual parsing over which are unwitting and The Watergate equivalent is claiming that Deep Throat was worse than Haldeman, Ehrlichman and Nixon.</p><div id=\"story-ad--aggro-6\" class=\"story-ad ad ad-placeholder nocontent robots-nocontent  ad-aggro-6 aggro-only\">\n    <div class=\"accessibility-ad-header visually-hidden\">\n    <p>Advertisement</p>\n</div>\n<a class=\"visually-hidden skip-to-text-link\" href=\"#story-continues-3\">Continue reading the main story</a>\n</div>\n<p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"498\" data-total-count=\"4082\" id=\"story-continues-3\">It fell to Adam Schiff, a Democratic representative from Southern California, to lay out the suspicious ties between Trump and Russia (while also hinting he couldn\u2019t describe some classified details). Schiff did so in a calm, nine-minute monologue that\u2019s <a href=\"https://thinkprogress.org/this-congressmans-short-speech-crisply-lays-out-the-evidence-of-collusion-between-trump-and-russia-d166801a11fa#.7swgnypku\">worth watching</a>. He walked through pro-Putin payments to Michael Flynn and through another Trump\u2019s aide\u2019s advance notice of John Podesta\u2019s hacked email and through the mysterious struggle over the Republican Party platform on Ukraine.</p><p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"492\" data-total-count=\"4574\">\u201cIs it possible that all of these events and reports are completely unrelated, and nothing more than an entirely unhappy coincidence? Yes, it is possible,\u201d Schiff said. \u201cBut it is also possible, maybe more than possible, that they are not coincidental, not disconnected and not unrelated, and that the Russians used the same techniques to corrupt U.S. persons that they have employed in Europe and elsewhere. We simply don\u2019t know, not yet, and we owe it to the country to find out.\u201d</p><button class=\"button comments-button  theme-speech-bubble-large\" data-skip-to-para-id=\"\">\n</button>\n<p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"253\" data-total-count=\"4827\">Comey, as much as liberals may <a href=\"https://www.nytimes.com/2016/11/13/us/politics/hillary-clinton-james-comey.html\">loathe</a> him for his 2016 bungling, seems to be one of the few public officials with the ability and willingness to pursue the truth. I dearly hope that Republican members of the Senate are patriotic enough to do so as well.</p><div id=\"story-ad--aggro-8\" class=\"story-ad ad ad-placeholder nocontent robots-nocontent  ad-aggro-4 ad-aggro-8 aggro-only\">\n    <div class=\"accessibility-ad-header visually-hidden\">\n    <p>Advertisement</p>\n</div>\n<a class=\"visually-hidden skip-to-text-link\" href=\"#story-continues-4\">Continue reading the main story</a>\n</div>\n<p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"81\" data-total-count=\"4908\" id=\"story-continues-4\">Our president is a liar, and we need to find out how serious his latest lies are.</p><footer class=\"story-footer story-content\">\n    <div class=\"story-meta\">\n                        <div class=\"story-info\"><p><em>You can join me on <a href=\"https://twitter.com/DLeonhardt\" title=\"Ross Douthat on Twitter\">Twitter (@DLeonhardt)</a> and <a href=\"https://www.facebook.com/DavidRLeonhardt/\">Facebook</a>.
