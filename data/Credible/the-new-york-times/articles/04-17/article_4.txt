I hope the president continues to play golf (I don\u2019t get those progressive critics who say Trump is ruining the world and then they complain because he takes time off). I hope his team continues to take advantage of the fact that it takes only one inexperienced stooge to undo the accomplishments of 100 normal workers.</p><p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"124\" data-total-count=\"4645\">And I hope it continues to negatively surpass all expectations. I remain a full-fledged member in the community of the agog.</p><button class=\"button comments-button  theme-speech-bubble-large\" data-skip-to-para-id=\"\">\n</button>\n<p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"189\" data-total-count=\"4834\">One of the things I\u2019ve learned about incompetence over the past few months is that it is radically nonlinear. Competent people go in one of a few directions. But incompetence is infinite.</p><p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"124\" data-total-count=\"4958\">The human imagination is not capacious enough to comprehend all the many ways the Trumpians can find to screw this thing up.</p><footer class=\"story-footer story-content\">\n    <div class=\"story-meta\">\n                        <div class=\"story-info\"><p><em>Follow
