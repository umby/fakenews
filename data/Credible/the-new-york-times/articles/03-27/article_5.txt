according to administration and congressional officials.</p><p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"476\" data-total-count=\"758\">The White House Counsel\u2019s Office was Flynn, who would briefly serve as the national security adviser.</p><p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"384\" data-total-count=\"1384\">Later that month, though, Mr. Kislyak requested a second meeting, which Mr. Kushner asked a deputy to attend in his stead, officials said.
